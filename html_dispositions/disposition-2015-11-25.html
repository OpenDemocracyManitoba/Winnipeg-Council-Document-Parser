Council Motions

# Movers Subject Disposition
Leave of Absence Browaty, Schreyer That Councillor Wyatt be granted a leave of absence from today’s meeting due to a personal family matter. CARRIED
1 Gerbasi, Pagtakhan That Item 4 of the Report of Executive Policy Committee dated November 18, 2015 be amended to delete Recommendation No. 1 and replace it with the following: “1. That consideration be given to changing jurisdiction over funding of the Public Art Strategy to the Standing Policy Committee on Protection and Community Services and Parks during the annual budget review.” CARRIED
2 Eadie, Schreyer That the Winnipeg Public Service work with the City of Winnipeg’s CUPE 500 representatives to provide a report on implementing cost saving measures identified in the CUPE 500 report describing a potential savings of $3,000,000.00. AUTOMATIC REFERRAL TO THE STANDING POLICY COMMITTEE ON INFRASTRUCTURE RENEWAL AND PUBLIC WORKS
3 Allard, Mayes That the administration report in 90 days on the implementation of free wifi services to the public in city of Winnipeg facilities AUTOMATIC REFERRAL TO THE STANDING POLICY COMMITTEE ON PROPERTY AND DEVELOPMENT, HERITAGE AND DOWNTOWN DEVELOPMENT
4 Eadie, Dobson That the Winnipeg Public Service provide a report to the SPC On Water and Waste, Riverbank Management and Environment about how a potential breach and contamination of the aqueduct can be dealt with to ensure the City of Winnipeg suffers limited damages; That this report describe the potential damages to the City of Winnipeg given a disastrous event where the six pipe line right of way intersects with the aqueduct. AUTOMATIC REFERRAL TO THE STANDING POLICY COMMITTEE ON WATER AND WASTE, RIVERBANK MANAGEMENT AND THE ENVIRONMENT
5 Mayes That the 2016 Council Meetings outlined on Appendices "A" and “B” be changed as follows: May 18 to May 25 June 15 to June 22 July 13 to July 20 LOST

  How This Document Was Created

This visualization of the 2015-11-25 Winnipeg City Council Disposition was created by Open Democracy Manitoba using information from the Winnipeg.ca Open Data Portal.

Dispositions are prepared by the City Clerks' Department using Microsoft Word. Tables are used in the Word Dispositions to structure the council meeting information. Word Dispositions are retrieved by Open Democracy Manitoba from the Winnipeg Open Data Portal and converted, first into JSON (a structured data format) and then into webpages like this one.
