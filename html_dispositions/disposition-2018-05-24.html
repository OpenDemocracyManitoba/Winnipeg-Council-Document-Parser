  Motions

Council Motions

# Movers Subject Disposition
1 Orlikow, Allard That the Declaration of Expropriation dated the 22nd day of February, 2018, made pursuant to By-law No. 13/2018 and expropriating the following lands: Firstly, All of the land contained within Lots 38 and 39 Plan 1631 WLTO Exc out of said Lot 38, Wly 207 feet in RL 4 to 7 Parish of St Vital Secondly, All of the land contained within Lots 1 and 47 Block 2 and Lot 1 Block 3 Plan 39145 WLTO in OTM Lots 4, 5, 6 and 7 Parish of St Vital IS HEREBY CONFIRMED. The lands described above are expropriated free of encumbrances, except for Instrument Number 2564287/1. The lands described above except: mines, minerals and named substances which are excepted from or not included in the Certificate of Title of the registered owner of the surface under The Real Property Act or are not owned by the owner of the surface under The Registry Act; and reservations in favour of the Crown as excepted from the Title of the owner of the surface, or to which Title is subject by implication under the provisions of The Real Property Act. CARRIED
2 Orlikow, Gerbasi THAT Recommendation 12 of Item 5 of the Report of the Standing Policy Committee on Property and Development Heritage and Downtown Development dated May 7, 2018 be amended by adding the following to the end of the recommendation: “and that the City of Winnipeg prepare the necessary documents within 90 days.” CARRIED
Condolence Pagtakhan, Schreyer THAT Council place on record its profound sorrow at the death of former Councillor Saul Cherniack, which occurred on March 30, 2018 CARRIED BY SILENT STANDING VOTE

  How This Document Was Created

This visualization of the 2018-05-24 Winnipeg City Council Disposition was created by Open Democracy Manitoba using information from the Winnipeg.ca Open Data Portal.

Dispositions are prepared by the City Clerks' Department using Microsoft Word. Tables are used in the Word Dispositions to structure the council meeting information. Word Dispositions are retrieved by Open Democracy Manitoba from the Winnipeg Open Data Portal and converted, first into JSON (a structured data format) and then into webpages like this one.
