  Motions

Council Motions

# Movers Subject Disposition
1 Nason, Klein That item 3 of the Report of the Executive Policy Committee dated June 18, 2020 be amended by deleting Recommendation 1 and replacing it with the following: 1.That the City Clerk be directed to provide all Members of Council with a link to view in camera meetings that are being conducted remotely, upon request of the Member. CARRIED
2 Gillingham, Browaty That item 1 of the Report of the Standing Policy Committee on Infrastructure Renewal and Public Works dated June 9, 2020 be amended by adding the following new Recommendation 2 and renumbering the remaining recommendation accordingly; “2.That the Winnipeg Parking Authority work with local business owners and report back to the appropriate committee of Council annually for 2 years on the impact on businesses of the on-street parking rate reduction.” CARRIED
3 Eadie, Schreyer That Recommendation 1 of item 4 of the Report of the Standing Policy Committee on Infrastructure Renewal and Public Works dated June 9, 2020 be amended by adding the following street as an additional expanded Sunday/holiday active transportation route with the same conditions as the existing nine routes; “J. Rover Avenue - Hallet Street to Stephens Street” CARRIED
4 Klein, Nason 1.That Recommendation 1 of item 4 of the Report of the Standing Policy Committee on Infrastructure Renewal and Public Works dated June 9, 2020 be amended by deleting Recommendation “1. E. Assiniboine Avenue – Bedson Street to Westwood Drive”, and renumbering the remaining recommendations accordingly. 2.That the following new Recommendation 2. be added, and the remaining recommendations be renumbered accordingly. “2. That the Winnipeg Public Service be requested to work with the Councillor of the Charleswood -Tuxedo - Westwood Ward to find an alternate temporary active transportation route within the area.” CARRIED
5 Chambers, Rollins That item 9 of the Report of the Executive Policy Committee dated June 18, 2020 be amended by adding the following new Recommendation 3 and renumbering the remaining recommendation accordingly: That Councillor Santos be appointed to the Winnipeg Police Board. CARRIED
LEAVE OF ABSENCE Rollins, Orlikow That Councillor Gilroy be excused from today’s meeting due to a personal family matter. CARRIED

  How This Document Was Created

This visualization of the 2020-06-26 Winnipeg City Council Disposition was created by Open Democracy Manitoba using information from the Winnipeg.ca Open Data Portal.

Dispositions are prepared by the City Clerks' Department using Microsoft Word. Tables are used in the Word Dispositions to structure the council meeting information. Word Dispositions are retrieved by Open Democracy Manitoba from the Winnipeg Open Data Portal and converted, first into JSON (a structured data format) and then into webpages like this one.
